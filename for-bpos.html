06BPO questions
For BPOs

The objections, answered.

Is DefrilexCX a competitor to BPOs?

No. DefrilexCX is a fulfillment partner. We provide the multilingual workforce capacity that BPOs deploy under their own brand to serve their enterprise clients. We do not compete for end client relationships.

How does the cost compare to bilingual FTE hiring?

Bilingual FTEs cost 30 to 50% more than monolingual agents and experience significantly higher attrition, especially in high demand languages. DefrilexCX converts fixed labor cost into variable delivery cost, typically at a significant reduction for high demand and hard to staff languages.

Can DefrilexCX support healthcare and government programs?

Yes. Our compliance infrastructure is designed for regulated environments. HIPAA BAA, Section 1557, and credentialing documentation are prepackaged and available for procurement review.

What if I only need overflow capacity during peak seasons?

Our model is specifically designed for flexible capacity. Seasonal peaks, enrollment surges, new client launches, we scale up and down based on your demand without fixed headcount commitments.

How does white label work in a BPO context?

DefrilexCX agents and interpreters operate under your brand, follow your scripts, use your protocols, and never reveal DefrilexCX’s involvement to your clients. We provide white label operating procedures and brand alignment documentation.
