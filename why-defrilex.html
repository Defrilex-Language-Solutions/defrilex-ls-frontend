01the shift that created us
Why Defrilex Language Solutions Marketplace

A federal mandate met a supply chain that couldn’t keep up.

Section 1557 of the Affordable Care Act, and the web of federal and state mandates around it, requires guaranteed, meaningful language access across healthcare and federally funded programs. The obligation is now legal, not optional.

But the interpreter supply chain never caught up. It is fragmented. Hard to staff languages go unfilled. Overnight and weekend coverage runs thin. The languages clients need most are the ones freelance networks supply least reliably.

So LSPs and BPOs now carry a compliance promise their staffing model can’t consistently keep. That gap is exactly where Defrilex Language Solutions operates.

02what we actually are
Why Defrilex Language Solutions Marketplace

Not a vendor. Not a marketplace. A fulfillment partner.

Defrilex Language Solutions is a Language Access Fulfillment Partner, a delivery execution layer that complements LSPs and BPOs rather than competing with them for the enterprise client relationship.

We are operations first, not tech first. We don’t sell software or dress staffing up as a platform. We deliver vetted interpreters, multilingual CX agents, and high demand language capacity that plugs into the delivery model you already run.

One promise sits underneath everything: we make language access easier to operate.
