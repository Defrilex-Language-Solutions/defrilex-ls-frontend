06LSP questions
For LSPs

The objections, answered.

Will Defrilex Language Solutions try to sell directly to my clients?

No. Defrilex Language Solutions is a white label fulfillment partner. We never contact, sell to, or build relationships with your end clients. Your clients see your brand, always.

What happens if Defrilex Language Solutions can’t fill a request?

We are transparent about coverage. Our proposals specify exactly which languages and hours we can cover at SLA grade reliability. We do not promise coverage we cannot deliver.

How does Defrilex Language Solutions compare to LanguageLine overflow?

Defrilex Language Solutions operates as a white label partner with a structurally lower cost base, particularly in rare and hard to staff languages, better margins for LSPs than incumbent overflow vendors while maintaining compliance grade quality.

Can Defrilex Language Solutions handle healthcare and government work?

Yes. Our compliance infrastructure is designed for regulated environments. HIPAA BAA, Section 1557, credentialing, and E&O insurance are prepackaged.

How quickly can a pilot start?

Most pilots are operational within 2 to 4 weeks of agreement. The scope is defined collaboratively, typically one language pair, one modality, and one coverage window.
